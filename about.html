WORK AND PASSION

Hi, my name is Angelika Cimene. I specialize in a variety of graphic design such as web design and development, UIUX, layout and print design, and branding. I attended University of California, Davis and received a bachelor’s degree in Design and a minor in Professional Writing in 2015.

I am currently a Graphic Designer for Spotlight Education but I am available for freelance work of all sorts. Contact me at angcim.designs@gmail.com

GET TO KNOW ME

My name is Angelika Cimene. I was born in the Philippines but I came to California when I was 6 years old and have lived in LA for most of my life. After graduating, I moved to Orlando, Fl for a new adventure.

My favorite hobbies include watching classic films and binge-watching tv shows. As a science fiction lover, my favorite movies are Back to the Future and Metropolis. I also enjoy photography, editing photos, and hand-lettering.

During my spare time, I run an instagram blog and youtube channel that contains all of my weekly adventures such as going to new places, trying out new foods and going to Disney World.

Feel free to follow my instagram, @happybaabes, and subscribe to my youtube channel, happybaabes
